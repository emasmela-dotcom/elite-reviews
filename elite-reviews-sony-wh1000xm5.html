What Could Be Better ü§î

1. No Folding Design

Unlike the XM4, these don't fold flat. They only swivel and collapse:

  • Carrying case is bulkier
  • Takes up more space in a bag
  • Less travel-friendly

Not a dealbreaker, but the XM4 was more portable.

2. Expensive

$399 is a lot for headphones. The XM4 (previous generation) are often $100-150 cheaper and still excellent.

3. Touch Controls Can Be Finicky

The touch-sensitive ear cups are convenient but sometimes:

  • Register accidental touches
  • Don't respond when adjusting headphones
  • Can be frustrating in winter with gloves

Physical buttons would be more reliable.

4. No IP Rating

These aren't water-resistant. Don't wear them in heavy rain or at the gym. For workouts, get something like Beats Fit Pro or AirPods Pro.
