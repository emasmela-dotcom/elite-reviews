What Could Be Better ü§î

1. It's HUGE

The 6.7" display is amazing but also massive:

  • Hard to use one-handed
  • Doesn't fit in small pockets
  • Can be awkward on phone calls

If you have smaller hands or want something more portable, get the regular Pro (6.1").

2. Price is High

$1,199 starting price is A LOT for a phone. And that's only for 256GB storage (128GB was removed this year). Want 1TB? That's $1,599!

3. Battery Life is Just "Good"

Don't get us wrong - it lasts all day. But considering the size, we expected better:

  • Light use: 1.5 days
  • Heavy use: Full day (barely)
  • Video playback: ~25 hours

The smaller iPhone 15 Pro somehow has similar battery life in testing.

4. Still No Always-On Display Customization

The Always-On Display looks great but drains battery faster than we'd like. We wish Apple let us turn off the wallpaper completely like Android phones.

Final Verdict

The iPhone 15 Pro Max is the best iPhone Apple has ever made, with top-tier performance, an incredible camera system, and premium titanium build quality. The 5x telephoto camera alone makes it worth considering over the regular Pro.

However, most people should get the iPhone 15 Pro (6.1") instead. It costs $200 less, fits better in pockets, and the 3x camera is still excellent. You're only missing out on the 5x zoom and a slightly bigger screen.

But if you love big screens, take lots of zoomed-in photos, or want the absolute best? The Pro Max is worth the extra money. Highly recommended.
